Our Games

Sudoku Game

Sudoku is a logic-based number puzzle that's both challenging and addictive. The game consists of a grid, which is divided into nine 3x3 subgrids. The objective is to fill the entire grid with numbers , ensuring that no number is repeated in any row, column, or subgrid.

Math Solver

Math Solver is an exciting game that challenges your mathematical skills. With three different levels of difficulty, you can choose to solve equations at your own pace or compete against others. The goal is to score the highest while solving equations as quickly as possible.

Sudoku Game

Instructions:

  1. Complete the puzzle by filling in empty spaces with numbers.
  2. Ensure that the same number does not occur more than once in the same row, column, and the 3x3 square.
  3. Choose your preferred grid size: 3x3, 6x6, or others.
  4. Adjust the number of empty squares to control the game's difficulty level.
  5. Have fun and enjoy playing Sudoku!
Math Solver

Instructions:

  • Test your math skills with three equations.
  • Solve the equations correctly and as quickly as possible to achieve a high rating.
  • Choose from three difficulty levels: easy, medium, and difficult.
  • Select the number of variables to use in solving the equations.
  • View your rating on the leaderboard at the bottom of the page to see how you rank among competitors.
  • Each username is allowed only one attempt to solve the problems.
  • If you want to play again, use a different username format (e.g., Ahmed1, Ahmed2).
  • Each username must be unique and not used previously.
